## üí° DELIVERY TIPS

### **Tone & Energy**
- **Confident but humble**: Speak with conviction, not arrogance
- **Enthusiastic**: Show passion for the problem you're solving
- **Clear and concise**: Every word counts in 60 seconds
- **Natural**: Don't sound scripted - make it conversational

### **Body Language**
- **Eye contact**: Look directly at camera (as if talking to a friend)
- **Smile**: Genuine, warm smile at the beginning
- **Hand gestures**: Use sparingly to emphasize key points
- **Posture**: Sit or stand straight, confident stance

### **Pacing**
- **Start strong**: First 10 seconds are critical - hook them immediately
- **Steady pace**: Don't rush, but don't drag
- **Pauses**: Brief pauses after key points for emphasis
- **End strong**: Clear ask and confident closing

### **Visuals (Optional)**
- **Screen recording**: Show 1-2 key features while talking (10-15 seconds)
- **Product demo**: Quick dashboard view during "What makes us different" section
- **Simple graphics**: Use text overlays for key numbers (customers, revenue, market size)

## üìù NOTES FOR RECORDING

1. **Practice 3-5 times** before recording
2. **Record in quiet space** with good lighting
3. **Use phone or laptop** - quality matters but not over-engineered
4. **Keep it natural** - if you mess up, pause and start the sentence again
5. **Time yourself** - aim for 55-60 seconds
6. **Show product** - If possible, include 10-15 seconds of product demo
7. **Be yourself** - Authenticity matters more than perfection
8. **Edit lightly** - Don't over-edit, keep it raw and real
